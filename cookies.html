Cookie Policy

Our use of cookies and similar technologies

Learn how we use cookies to enhance your browsing experience

This Cookie Policy explains how AquaGarden Care ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website. By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree to our use of cookies, you should adjust your browser settings accordingly or refrain from using our website.

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the website owners. This policy describes the types of cookies we use, the purposes for using them, and how you can manage your cookie preferences.

Types of Cookies We Use

1 Essential Cookies

Essential cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. These cookies enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility. Without these cookies, certain features of the website may not work correctly. For example, we use session cookies to keep you logged in as you navigate between pages. These cookies do not collect personal information and are typically deleted when you close your browser.

2 Performance Cookies

Performance cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. These cookies allow us to count visits and traffic sources so we can measure and improve site performance. They help us know which pages are most popular and how visitors move around the site. These cookies don't collect information that identifies a visitor personally. We use services like Google Analytics to implement these cookies.

3 Functionality Cookies

Functionality cookies enable the website to remember choices you make (such as your username, language preference, or region) and provide enhanced, more personal features. These cookies may be set by us or by third-party providers whose services we've added to our pages. The information these cookies collect may be anonymized and they cannot track your browsing activity on other websites. For instance, they might store your preferred currency for pricing display.

4 Targeting/Advertising Cookies

Targeting cookies may be set through our site by our advertising partners. They may be used by those companies to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant advertisements on other sites. They work by uniquely identifying your browser and internet device. If you do not allow these cookies, you will experience less targeted advertising. These cookies might store information like what pages you visit or how long you spend on different sections of our website.

5 First-Party Cookies

First-party cookies are set directly by us when you visit our website. These are used to provide a better user experience and remember your preferences. For example, we might use a first-party cookie to remember that you've accepted our cookie policy so you don't see the notification on every visit. First-party cookies are generally considered less of a privacy concern than third-party cookies because they can only be read by us.

Cookie Duration

6 Session Cookies

Session cookies are temporary cookies that remain on your device until you close your web browser. These enable our website to link your actions during a browser session, such as remembering items in your shopping cart or form information you've entered. Session cookies are essential for certain website features to work properly and are usually deleted automatically when you close your browser. They don't collect information from your computer or store any personally identifiable information.

7 Persistent Cookies

Persistent cookies remain on your device for the period of time specified in the cookie or until you manually delete them. These cookies help us recognize you as a returning visitor and remember your preferences, providing a more personalized experience. For example, they might remember your language preference so you don't have to select it every time you visit. Persistent cookies typically have expiration dates ranging from several days to a few years from the date they were created.

Third-Party Cookies

8 Analytics Cookies

We use third-party analytics cookies, such as those provided by Google Analytics, to gather anonymous information about how visitors use our website. These cookies collect information like the number of visitors to the site, what pages they visit, and how long they stay on each page. This helps us understand user behavior and improve our website's performance. The data collected is aggregated and anonymous, meaning we cannot identify individual visitors through these cookies.

9 Social Media Cookies

Our website may include social media features like Facebook "Like" buttons or Twitter widgets. These features may set cookies that allow the social media companies to track your visit to our site and may be used for their own purposes according to their privacy policies. These cookies enable you to share content directly to social media platforms and help us measure the effectiveness of our social media activities. You should review the relevant social media sites' cookie policies for more information.

10 Advertising Partner Cookies

We work with advertising partners who may set cookies when you visit our website to serve you with relevant ads on other websites. These cookies collect information about your browsing habits to show you ads more relevant to your interests. They also help prevent you from seeing the same ad repeatedly and measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns. You can opt out of interest-based advertising through the Digital Advertising Alliance's consumer choice tools.

Your Cookie Choices

11 Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. You can typically set your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you disable cookies entirely, some features of our website may not function properly. Each browser manages cookie controls differently, so visit your browser's help menu for specific instructions. Common browsers like Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and Internet Explorer all provide cookie management features.

12 Opt-Out Tools

Several organizations provide opt-out tools that allow you to control the collection and use of information for interest-based advertising. These include the Digital Advertising Alliance's opt-out tool (available in the US, Canada, and Europe) and the Network Advertising Initiative's opt-out tool. By using these tools, you can opt out of targeted advertising from participating companies. However, opting out does not mean you will no longer see ads; it simply means that the ads you see may be less relevant to your interests.

13 Cookie Consent Management

You can manage your cookie preferences through our cookie consent management tool, which allows you to accept or decline specific categories of cookies. This tool is available on our website and can be accessed at any time. By adjusting your preferences, you can control which types of cookies are set on your device while visiting our site.

14 Third-Party Cookie Management

If you wish to manage third-party cookies, you will need to visit the respective websites of those third parties. Many third-party advertisers provide their own opt-out mechanisms, which can be found on their websites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies and cookie management options to understand how they handle your data.
