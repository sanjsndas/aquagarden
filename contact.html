Comprehensive Contact Information

1 Consultation Requests

Schedule a comprehensive garden assessment with our certified consultants by calling our dedicated line or filling out the contact form. Our team will arrange a convenient time to visit your property, evaluate your current landscape, and discuss your vision. Initial consultations typically last 90 minutes and include a detailed examination of soil conditions, sun exposure patterns, and existing irrigation systems.

2 Service Inquiries

For questions about our specialized services including precision drip irrigation, artistic pruning, or seasonal maintenance programs, contact our service department during business hours. Our knowledgeable staff can explain service packages, scheduling options, and pricing structures. We recommend having basic information about your property ready, including square footage of garden areas and current irrigation methods if applicable.

3 Emergency Services

For urgent garden emergencies such as broken irrigation lines damaging property or hazardous tree situations, call our 24/7 emergency line at +1 (212) 555-0188. Our rapid response team can typically be on site within 3 hours in Manhattan and 6 hours in surrounding boroughs. Note that emergency service rates differ from standard service pricing and may include after-hours premiums.
